Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 03-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund.

Do you offer product warranties?

Yes, we offer a one-year warranty on all our products. If you experience any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Yes, you can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?

No, we only accept one payment method per order. If you want to split the payment, you will need to place separate orders.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team. They will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions on how to return your item.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes, we are committed to sustainability and offer a range of eco-friendly products. Look for the eco-friendly label on our product pages.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please check our shipping policy for more details.
